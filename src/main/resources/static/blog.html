The importance of health in Costa Rica

Nutrition goes beyond following a diet, seeing numbers on the scale, how thin or muscular we look.


Nutrition is OUR ALLY, it is a powerful tool that allows us to improve our quality of life, prevent diseases such as obesity, hypertension, dyslipidemia, diabetes.


Through nutrition we protect our immune system, allowing optimal functioning, since through the intake of nutrients we help our body fight infections.


Nutrition must be balanced, sufficient, adequate and complete, rich in foods of plant origin: fruit, vegetables, cereals, preferably whole grains, legumes and nuts, as well as dairy products, meat such as chicken, eggs and fish.
